Top 4 Things People Did During Lockdown

By: Victor Victorio

The Coronavirus Pandemic in 2020 led to a worldwide shutdown in efforts to reduce the spread. Due to its contagious nature, people were forced to lockdown in quarantine, forcing people to change the way they live their daily lives. During lockdown many people managed to find new ways to keep themselves busy. Some of the things that people did was streaming videos and movies, exercising at home, shopping online, and ordering take-out on popular delivery apps.
